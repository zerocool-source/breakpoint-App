On the Service Technicians page, in the technician detail panel (right side), add three new buttons next to the "+ Add Property" button:

**ADD THESE BUTTONS IN A ROW:**

1. "+ Add Property" (keep existing - green button)

2. "Extended Cover" button:
   - Ocean blue (#0077b6) outline style with calendar icon
   - When clicked, open a modal to set up extended coverage:
     - Select date range (From date - To date)
     - Select who will cover (dropdown of other technicians)
     - Select "Full Route" or "Select Specific Properties" (if specific, show checkboxes for each property)
     - Reason field (text)
     - "Cancel" and "Save Coverage" buttons
   - This creates coverage for this technician's route during the selected dates

3. "Split Route" button:
   - Orange (#f97316) outline style with split/fork icon
   - When clicked, open a modal to split the route:
     - Shows the technician who is out at the top
     - Two columns: "Technician A" and "Technician B" dropdowns to select covering techs
     - Below, show the properties from this tech's route
     - Allow dragging/moving properties to assign to Technician A or Technician B
     - Reason field
     - "Cancel" and "Split Route" buttons
   - This divides the route between two other technicians

4. "Lock Route" toggle button:
   - Toggle switch style or button with lock/unlock icon
   - When ON/Locked:
     - Shows locked padlock icon with "Route Locked" text
     - The technician cannot reorder or adjust their route in their mobile app
     - They must service properties in the exact order shown
   - When OFF/Unlocked:
     - Shows unlocked padlock icon with "Route Unlocked" text
     - The technician can adjust their route order in their app
   - Toggle changes status immediately with toast notification: "Route locked for [Tech Name]" or "Route unlocked for [Tech Name]"

**BUTTON STYLING:**
- Arrange buttons in a row below the "Properties" and "Notes" tabs
- Keep buttons compact and aligned
- Use icons to save space:
  - Add Property: + icon (green)
  - Extended Cover: calendar icon (ocean blue)
  - Split Route: split/fork icon (orange)
  - Lock Route: padlock icon (gray/red when locked, gray when unlocked)

**POSITIONING:**
- Place the button row above the "X assigned properties" text
- Or create a small toolbar/action bar with all four options
