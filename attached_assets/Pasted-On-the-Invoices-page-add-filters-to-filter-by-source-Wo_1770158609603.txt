**FILTER FUNCTIONALITY:**

When "Work Order" is selected from Sources filter:
- Show only invoices that have the "Work Order" tag
- Update the invoice count (e.g., "8 Invoices" instead of "25 Invoices")

When a technician is selected:
- Show only invoices assigned to that technician
- Works in combination with other filters
