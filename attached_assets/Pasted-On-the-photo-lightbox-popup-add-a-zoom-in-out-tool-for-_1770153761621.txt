On the photo lightbox/popup, add a zoom in/out tool for the images:

**ADD ZOOM FUNCTIONALITY:**

**1. ZOOM CONTROLS:**
- Add zoom in (+) button
- Add zoom out (-) button
- Add reset/fit to screen button
- Display current zoom level (e.g., "100%", "150%", "200%")

**2. ZOOM METHODS:**
- Click zoom in button: Increase zoom by 25% each click
- Click zoom out button: Decrease zoom by 25% each click
- Mouse scroll wheel: Zoom in/out
- Double-click on image: Toggle between fit-to-screen and 100% zoom
- Pinch to zoom on touch devices

**3. PAN/DRAG WHEN ZOOMED:**
- When image is zoomed in beyond the container, allow click and drag to pan around the image
- Show grab cursor when hovering over zoomed image
- Show grabbing cursor when dragging

**ZOOM CONTROL STYLING:**
- Buttons: White background, dark icons, rounded corners
- Position: Top of lightbox, near the X close button
- Zoom level display: Show percentage (50%, 100%, 150%, 200%, etc.)
- Min zoom: 50%
- Max zoom: 300%
- Default: Fit to screen

**KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:**
- `+` or `=` key: Zoom in
- `-` key: Zoom out
- `0` key: Reset to fit
- Arrow keys: Pan when zoomed in
- ESC: Close lightbox
