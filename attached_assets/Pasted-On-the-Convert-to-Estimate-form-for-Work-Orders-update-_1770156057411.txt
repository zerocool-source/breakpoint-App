**FEATURES:**

1. **Separate Before and After sections:**
   - "BEFORE" header with row of thumbnail images
   - "AFTER" header with row of thumbnail images
   - Each section can have multiple photos (up to 10 per section)

2. **Horizontal scrolling row:**
   - Photos displayed in a horizontal scrollable row
   - Each thumbnail ~120px Ã— 90px
   - Caption below each photo
   - [+] button at the end to add more photos

3. **Add Photos functionality:**
   - Click [+] to upload more photos to that section
   - Accept PNG, JPEG, HEIC formats
   - Max 10MB per photo

4. **Photo interactions:**
   - Click any photo to open large lightbox view
   - Lightbox shows all photos with navigation arrows
   - Zoom controls in lightbox
   - Delete button on hover to remove photo

5. **Sample data for Work Orders:**
   - WO-26-00001 (Palm Court): 3 Before photos, 2 After photos
   - WO-26-00002 (Sunset Hills): 2 Before photos, 2 After photos
   - etc.
