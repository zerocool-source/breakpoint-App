**STYLING:**
- Controls bar: Dark semi-transparent background (like current), rounded corners
- Position: Absolute, top right corner of the image container
- Margin: Small gap between controls and image edge (8-12px)
- Z-index: Above the image
- Keep the same button styling (âˆ’, %, +, Reset, X)

**CSS EXAMPLE:**
```css
.lightbox-controls {
  position: absolute;
  top: 10px;
  right: 10px;
  z-index: 10;
  background: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.7);
  border-radius: 8px;
  padding: 8px 12px;
  display: flex;
  align-items: center;
  gap: 8px;
}
```
